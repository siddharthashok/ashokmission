About

The philosophy of Buddha appears to be very important in our practical life and in wide national context. So, Ashoka the great, the high souled man who brought down philosophy of Buddha in the conduct of our life, society and the state, is an ideal man. We are going to build a nation in the light of the philosophy of Buddha, our guiding star and the most befitting ideal in the present context.

Ashok Mission Educational Society aims to impart scientific education to children in the light of Lord Buddha and great Ashoka’s preachings and dhamma.

Our mission is to take the help of education for the renewal and rejuvenation of our nation through education of our young children. The sowing of this seed of principle in the mind of children will offer them a different vision of life.

Expert and talented students are valuable assets to their family, society and nation. The mission aims to prepare the little ones for being a true nationalists with high values and morals.

The atmosphere for developing a wide vision of life of children during infancy has been kept in view in our school. In the developing stage , the students need games, sport library, reading room, cultural programmes . Our mission is alive to these needs of children. We encourage the cultural literary and promising writings.

We aim to prepare the children for a classless and casteless society. In order to make education of the talented children liberal and meaningful, our mission is promise-bound.
